Arts and health 🖼️🎵🏥

How does viewing visual art impact our wellbeing? May 2024 - present A growing number of studies show that making art is good for our health, but we know much less about the evidence landscape surrounding how viewing visual art (i.e., "receptive engagement") could impact our wellbeing. That's why I, together with a research team led by Marcel Foster, am scoping the global literature on this question. I've supported our team in searching for, screening, extracting data from, and synthesising the available evidence, and I'm looking forward to sharing our outputs.
